Biography

William Henry Gates III is an American business magnate, software developer, investor, and philanthropist. He is a co-founder of Microsoft Corporation, the world's largest personal-computer software company.

Gates held the positions of chairman, CEO, and chief software architect at Microsoft and was the largest individual shareholder until May 2014. He is also known for his philanthropic work through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, which is one of the world’s largest private charitable foundations.
